Word's frequencies vizualizer

This application provides data visualtization of words' frequencies and its relative frequencies in classic novel of manners Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen. Circles represent most common words in the book; just hover the mouse over a circle and you will see the most frequent words encountered after the original word (the deeper the red hue - the more frequently word been in the textâ€¨).

On the right side you have controls to tweak appearance of the graph. Moreover you can provide any text file for analysis of the same kind.
