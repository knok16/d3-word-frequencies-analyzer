Word's frequencies vizualizer

This application provides data visualtization of word's frequencies and its relative frequencies in classic novel of manners Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen. Circles represents most common words in book; just hover mouse over one circle and you will see most frequent words encountered after original word (the more circle is red - the more frequent word it represents).

On the right side you have controls to amend appearance of the graph. Moreover you can provide any text file for analysis of the same kind.
